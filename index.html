ABOUT US



EjoHeza is a Long-Term Saving Scheme established by
the Rwandan Government through the Ministry of Finance.
It operates as a defined contribution scheme,
administered by the Rwanda Social Security
Board (RSSB), catering to both salaried and unsalaried individuals
